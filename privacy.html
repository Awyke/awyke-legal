Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

Awyke ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Awyke.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and name to manage your account and sync your alarms across devices.

Device Data: Awyke uses motion sensors (accelerometers) on your device to detect movement during "Missions." This data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless specifically required for bug reporting.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics regarding app performance and feature usage to help us improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to maintain clinical-grade alarm reliability, process your subscriptions, and improve Awyke functions.

3. Data Storage

Your alarm settings and "Mission" preferences are stored locally on your device and synced via encrypted cloud storage associated with your Awyke account.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to process subscriptions and Firebase for secure authentication and database management. These services have their own privacy policies regarding your data.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time within the app settings. Deleting your account will remove all personal data from our active databases.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: awyke@refreege.app
